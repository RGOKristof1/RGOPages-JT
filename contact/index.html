How long does a typical project take? ›

It depends on scope. A landing page or small web app typically takes 2–4 weeks. A full platform with backend, database design, and custom admin can run 8–16 weeks. We'll give you a firm estimate after the discovery phase — no vague ranges.

Do you work with clients outside Hungary? ›

Yes — the majority of our work is remote. We've worked with clients across Europe and beyond. Time zones and language are rarely an issue; all project communication is in English.

What happens after launch? ›

Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support. Beyond that, we offer retainer arrangements for ongoing maintenance, feature development, and monitoring. We don't disappear after delivery.
